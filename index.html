Undergraduate Researcher at UIUC

• May 2025 - Present

Process ALMA IRS3B continuum and C17O line data in CASA by performing calibration checks, continuum subtraction, and multiscale tclean() imaging to generate refined FITS cubes and moment maps. Develop reproducible imaging pipelines that adjust masks, multiscale parameters, and Briggs/uv-taper settings to enhance faint spiral and infall features. Use smoothed cubes and extracted PV diagrams to study dust–gas morphology, axis-dependent asymmetries, and inward-bending velocity structures—key evidence for the “badminton-birdie” infall pattern reported in IRS3B.
